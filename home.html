Welcome to

Mabroc Tea

Supplying the finest pure Ceylon tea to the world started as a dream by a small group of Sri Lankan tea lovers back in 1988. Today, Mabroc not only supplies pure Ceylon tea to over 50 countries, but is also the 8th largest tea exporter in the country. Over 12.5 million kilos of premium Ceylon tea are grown each year in our 9,605 hectares of pristine tea gardens and processed in 47 tea factories before making their way into the world.

We ensure quality on every step of its journey from tea bud to taste bud.

That’s why our pure Ceylon tea is talked about.

Unsurprisingly, so is our range of aromatic organic spices – for which we’re justifiably famous - including Cloves, Cinnamon, white & black Pepper and Mace.

And our herbs are being whispered about too by those who appreciate the finest ingredients in their cooking and medicinal treatments.
